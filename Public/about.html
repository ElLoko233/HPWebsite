Our Story

SHPS was just a dream laying dormant in the minds of Mrs.Peens and Mr.Motsoahae. It is a dream inspired by the excellent performance of the 2018 matriculants from the main school, Sunward Park High. Impressed by their results, they began to wonder what would happen if they brought the best of the best into one school and let them share their intellectual ability amongst each other. That is when Mrs.Peens and Mr.Motsoahae agreed to establish Sunward High Performance School.

However, achieving such an ambitious dream was not a walk in the park. The founders had to battle through the struggle of organizing a place within Sunward that could facilitate more or less than 200 learners; in addition to funding for the school's needs and getting enough quality educators. The toughest challenge that they had to face was overcoming the drowning ocean of anxiety that accompanied the uncertainty of not knowing how many students would enroll. As the opening day got closer, the fear of not having a student to teach, got louder. Their anxieties deprived them of a peaceful night's rest. However, the opposite of their torment was a reality. Numerous parents and children believed in their dream and they were determined to seize it and make it their own. Before the school opened, more than 200 learners were enrolling.

On 15 January 2020, Sunward High Performance School welcomed its first group of raw brainpower. On their first year they focused on establishing the school's culture, refining the staff, and allowing the students to shape their own rules and responsibilities that will govern them and future learners. Then in the following year, Sunward High Performance School had its first group of matriculants. Through the hard work, devotion, and love for their students, the educators at SHPS were able to enrich their learner's minds and sharpen their thinking. All of which enabled them to perform incredibly well in their final exams thus allowing them to obtain a 100% passing rate and 90% of them leaving with a bachelor's pass.

Our Mission

Provide students with quality learning and teaching with the assistance of our advanced e-learning equipment.

By nurturing the values of curiosity, creativity, collaboration, courage, and confidence; we are committed to enabling our students to become global citizens.

Produce university-ready students by exposing them to a learning environment of elevated expectations in their performance.

How will we achieve our mission?

Teaching is one of the most important jobs in the world, and we want to do it right. To guarantee that Sunward High Performance School fulfills its mission, we are doing our very best to integrate the efficient world of technology by replacing classic school equipment with better and effective electronics. We use smart screens as the main teaching system, replacing the old chalkboard with a fully interactive screen that can be used for anything from displaying a lesson to watching videos. To make learning more convenient, our learners have laptops that contain all their textbooks and other learning material, which they can access anytime they want, anywhere they want - it's like having an entire library at their fingertips. It's no secret that maintaining a small class size provides a more personalized learning experience for students. At SHPS, this means fewer distractions and more one-on-one time with your teacher.
